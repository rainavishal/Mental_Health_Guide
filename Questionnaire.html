Ques1 : Have you been having any trouble concentrating on daily tasks?

  1. I have been failing to meet deadlines.
  2. I am feeling overwhelmed by my daily tasks
  3. I have no motivation to start projects on time
  4. I avoid doing the task at hand as I know it may be more challenging

Ques2 : Do you feel disheartened when you look at yourself daily while dressing up?

  1. I feel dissatisfied when I look at myself and wish I was skinny/thinner
  2. Does not being the correct weight limit distress you daily.
  3. I avoid social situations with my friends/family due to how I look
  4. I feel my confidence has shattered due to how I look and avoid any situations outside my comfort zone

Ques3 : Do you like being alone or does it cause any long-lasting sadness?

  1. I am introvert but miss being a little social
  2. Staying in self isolation makes me feel distant
  3. I am unable to make new friends.
  4. I am feeling lonely and am experiencing long periods of sadness.

Ques4 : How do you feel when you indulge in intoxicating yourself?

  1. Intoxicating myself gives the sense of peace that I do not feel when sober.
  2. I feel excited and like doing it
  3. Being intoxicated makes my feeling of anxiety go away.
  4. Smoking a pack a day or being extremely drunk makes my stress go away

Ques5 : Did you face any injury to your body in past 4 weeks? Do you feel any pain?

  1. I have recovered physically and mentally from the pain
  2. I have not recovered and am currently facing physical pain
  3. I have recovered but am not mentally ready to perform tasks without being scared of feeling pain
  4. Feeling pain has caused me to feel more frustrated than usual and makes me feel immobilized/paralyzed.

Ques6 : Does any sudden sounds startle you extremely or interfere with work and role functioning?

  1. Loud noises disturb me but do not impair my ability to do daily work
  2. I feel extremely disturbed , irritated and frustrated at loud noises
  3. I avoid going outside during festivals involving loud speakers and other triggers
  4. I am unable to think after being triggered and feel paranoia, restlessness and paralyzed.

Ques7 : Have you been experiencing sleepless night

  1. I get a complete 7/8 hours of sleep
  2. I feel stressed and am constantly worried about everything while sleeping
  3. I am unable to calm my mind and relax my body while preparing to go to sleep
  4. I feel short-bursts of paranoia and experience panic attacks as I feel someone is watching me

Ques8 : Have you experienced any sort of hazy illusions?

  1. I get a complete 7/8 hours of sleep
  2. I can hear unusual sounds in my ears like clicking, banging and clapping and also see weird figures around me
  3. I experienced an increased number of nightmares that occur due to paranoia
  4. I have a regular sleep schedule and do not experience any illusions

Ques9 : Have you felt like an unnecessary load on others?

  1. I do not feel as such
  2. I have been feeling distant and disinterested in everything around me
  3. I feel like a failure due to my lack of achievement regarding my goals
  4. I have given up and feel it would be easier to give away every prized possession and commit suicide
